About me.

I hold a Master's in Psychology from the University of Bristol and a PhD from France, and have lived and worked in Taiwan, the UK, France, Belgium, and Germany. This multicultural journey shapes everything I do. Based in Berlin, I specialize in helping professionals with multicultural backgrounds navigate the emotional and behavioural challenges of living across environments.

With a background in cognitive neuroscience, my approach blends evidence-based CBT with insights from how the brain learns, adapts, and heals. Combined with intercultural awareness, this creates a space where your cultural background, your neurobiology, and your current reality are all honoured.

What I can help with.

Become who you were always meant to be.

By understanding how we think, we can change how we behave. And by changing our behaviour, we learn from it and reshape the way we think. This is the cognitive cycle at the heart of CBT. Combined with neuroscience insights and intercultural awareness, we go beyond coping strategies to create lasting change. It's not about fixing you. It's about helping you find your way.
